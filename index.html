Heal the Whole.










Trauma can be a masterful teacher. Never letting that trauma define who you are, but at the same time honoring the way it shapes you is a fine balancing act indeed. Kyla takes her one-on-one experience with death and uses it to help and heal those around her.Once she found her path out…she never looked back. She watched as her life transformed around her, and even though she struggles to accept the beauty the universe has blessed her with through the love of her husband and one-year old son, she can never forget how she arrived at this place.


She is a shaman, a priestess, a wind-walker who feels a deep connection to the people and all things around her. Kyla is an ordained minister and a Reiki practitioner who has studied Arts Management at the University of Tulsa (2006), and a Recovery Support Specialist with an emphasis on substance abuse and trauma (Oklahoma State University, 2016).

Kyla’s spiritual studies are primarily focused on the chakras, animal medicine, the ways of our ancestors, feminism, and the divine harmony between everything on Earth and beyond. She is both spiritual and analytical, and she brings her authentic perspective to all her work.
